Scary Facts about the Nasty Evil Death Star

The Superlaser is the Death Star's main weapon.

The Superlaser is created by coordinating eight lasers manned by 168 gunners located in the Eye of the ship.

The Eye of the ship is a circular concave shape built around a focusing crystal.

The Eye has no defenses and requires military backup for protection.

The Equatorial Trench is the large deep channel along the equator of the vessel.
It contains many of the main systems including:
Landing bays, drive thrusters and tractor beams.

The surface of the Death Star is covered with thousands of buildings
so it looks like a massive city.

Mooring Towers dock large capital ships, from which the malicious malingering commander would sometimes control the fleet.
